Services Provided by Our Hospital

We offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services to meet the needs of our patients.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services to handle critical and urgent medical conditions.

Outpatient Services

General consultations, follow-ups, and specialist appointments without the need for hospital admission.

Diagnostic Services

Advanced diagnostic tools including MRI, CT scan, X-rays, and laboratory testing for accurate diagnoses.

Surgical Services

Wide range of surgical procedures, from minor operations to complex surgeries with experienced surgeons.

Maternity and Child Care

Comprehensive prenatal, birthing, and postnatal care, along with pediatric services.

Pharmacy

Fully stocked pharmacy with prescription medications and over-the-counter drugs available on-site.

Rehabilitation and Physiotherapy

Physical therapy, rehabilitation, and pain management services to help patients recover and regain mobility.

Cardiology Services

Advanced cardiac care including diagnostics, treatment, and management of heart conditions.

Oncology Services

Comprehensive cancer care including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and support services.
